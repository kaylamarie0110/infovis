Hampton Roads: In- & Out-Flow of DoD Contracting Dollars

Kayla Henneman & Swaraj Wankhade


Description

Because DoD contracting is a large part of Hampton Roads' economy, it is important to know how the contracting money flows in and out of the area. For this reason, our project looks to discover trends in the DoD contracting dollar money flow of Hampton Roads, such as: netflow of contracting dollars, where the money flows to if it is leaving here, and where the money comes from if it coming to Hampton Roads. To this end, we created a Bubble Map of the United States, where hue is used to encode type of flow and size of bubble is used to encode the amount of dollars flowing to or from a particular county. We also keep two line graphs next to the map so the user always has an overview of how the inflows and outflows have changed overtime. In addition, when a user hovers over a county the name of the county and amount of inflow/outflow dollars is shown and the line graphs change to show only data for that county over time.
